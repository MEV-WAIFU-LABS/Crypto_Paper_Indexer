NISQ Algorithm Zoo

About
A curated list of algorithm implementations on noisy, intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) computers. Implementations appear in reverse chronological order and include architecture (e.g., superconducting), number of qubits, algorithm(s), and a reference.
