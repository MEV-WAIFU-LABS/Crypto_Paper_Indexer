Privacy policy

NISQ Algorithm Zoo is a free resource supported by Unitary Fund. This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

To create an account we collect your username, email and password (that is stored encrypted).
To be included in our weekly digest mailing list we just collect your email address.
We use Google Analytics to analyse the patterns of usage, but we do not connect any personally identifiable information (such as username or email) to individual sessions. So we only store the anonymous behaviour data.
